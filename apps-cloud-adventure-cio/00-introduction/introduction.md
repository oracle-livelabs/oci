# Introduction

## About this AI Adventure

Oracle AI Adventure in SaaS Platform & Technology is an interactive, hands-on experience that lets you explore the latest Oracle SaaS capabilities that allow application owners to deliver user centric application experiences and AI-power the enterprise. 
<br><br>
Experience how Oracle’s built-in AI unlocks new efficiencies with digital co-workers, elevates user experience, transforms data into actionable insights, and enhances decision-making, security, and productivity.
<br><br>
Estimated Adventure Time: 2 hours

### **Objectives**

This Activity Guide will lead you along the way through a set of Adventure Labs

    > (1) **Access Control**: Users require access to application features and functionality, including AI driven ones. Experience the ease of adding additional access privileges and capabilities to an application user.<br>

    > (2) **Risk Management Controls**: Enterprises need to manage and mitigate risks associated with business operations. Experience using Oracle Risk Management Cloud to enable capabilities to manage role request approvals and provide notification of segregation of duties violations.<br>

    > (3) **Application UI Configuration**: Experience the power of configuration capabilities of Oracle Fusion Cloud Applications to accommodate your business requirements and empower end users to do their job effectively.<br>

    > (4) **Application Extension**: Oracle Fusion Cloud offers powerful extension capabilities natively within the application. Experience the ability to create new application pages that enhance end user productivity.<br>

    > (5) **AI Agent Studio**: Experience Oracle’s AI Agent Studio and see how AI is changing enterprise applications. Leverage AI Agent Studio’s delivered agent templates, create AI Agents and AI Agent Teams, and deploy Agents for automating business processes.<br>

    > (6) **Analytics**: Learn how Fusion Data Intelligence (FDI) can provide a unified view and meaningful insights into your company’s operations. You will get hands-on experience in answering questions regarding your enterprise health by leveraging pre-built AI insights and creating cross-functional analytics across different lines of business.<br>
